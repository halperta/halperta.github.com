First Year Job Materials

  Here are some materials used during my first year on the job market (2016). I was selected for first round interviews for these jobs, so I guess I did something right, though most didn't lead to offers. These are intended to be helpful examples, but they are so, so far from perfect, so please seek out other info before you use them! (And please don't cite/circulate these docs as if they were published works - they're just templates at this point.)
