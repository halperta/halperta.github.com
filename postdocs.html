Postdoctoral Laborers

Welcome

  Postdoctoral Laborers is an informal community of postdocs with training in the humanities. We hold regular virtual and in-person meetings to build community, help one another navigate the postdoctoral world, and create documentation for postdocs and their supervisors.

Join the group

This group is primarily for current postdocs and those on the postdoctoral market. The group meets virtually at 12pt/3et on the first Monday of every month. To join the Slack Channel or email list, contact Hannah Alpert-Abrams (halperta@gmail.com)

We also work with HASTAC to organize public events. Join our HASTAC group: https://www.hastac.org/groups/postdoctoral-laborers

If you are a former postdoc or a supervisor, contact us to find out more about how you can get involved.
