Research Summary 4

Back to “Assignments”

Assignment Overview

Write a one-page (8.5x11) paper analyzing the logical appeals in one of the articles from essay 1 (or a new position article relating to your controversy). Put your name in the top left hand corner and list the author’s name and the full citation information of the piece you’re summarizing; set margins at 1 inch, spacing at single, and font at 12 pt. Do not exceed a page in length. This should be about 500 words.

Finding a Source

Choose an article that you think is rhetorically interesting - one that is particularly well written and susbtantive, one that is extremely persuasive, or one that you consider to be excessively biased. You can use an article from essay 1, or pick a new article related to your controversy.

Specifics

Your research summary should have three parts.

In the first part of the summary (1 paragraph), briefly state your controversy and identify the position that the author of this article taking.

In the second (and largest) part of your summary, identify three logical appeals that are made in the article. Describe the appeal in the format ”” or ”.” For each logical appeal, explain what assumptions the appeal relies on.

In the third part of the summary (1 paragraph), briefly explain whether or not this article relies upon logical appeals to make its point. Consider the following questions: Does it contain many logical appeals? Does it contain many logical fallacies (things that look like logic, but aren’t!)? Does the article seem to be centered around logical arguments, or are the arguments less important than other things like emotions?
